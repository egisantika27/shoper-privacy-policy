Privacy Policy for SHOPER

Effective Date: August 27, 2025

Thank you for using SHOPER ("Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, and how we use, share, and safeguard it.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of the Extension, we only collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you register for a trial account or log in, we collect your email address and password. Your password is securely stored in encrypted format.
  • License Information: When you activate a premium license, we store a record of that activation linked to your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • User Authentication: To verify your identity during login and to secure your account.
  • License Management: To manage your subscription status, whether a trial period or a premium license.
  • Important Communication: We may use your email address to send essential information related to your account, such as payment confirmations, trial expiration notices, or important service updates.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

To maximize your privacy, we explicitly do not collect, store, or track the following data:

  • Your browsing history on Shopee or other websites.
  • Products you view, search, or analyze.
  • Personal information from your Shopee account.
  • Any data you input into the extension’s filters.

All research data you perform stays within your browser and is never sent to our servers.

4. Information Sharing

We will never share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third party.

Your information is securely stored using services from Supabase, Inc. as our backend infrastructure provider. Supabase adheres to strict security and data privacy standards.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Extension may include links to third-party services, such as Mayar, for processing license purchases. When you choose to make a payment through these services, you will be redirected to their respective platforms.

  • We do not collect, store, or process any payment details (such as credit card numbers or banking information).
  • All payment-related information is handled solely by Mayar under their own Privacy Policy.
  • We encourage you to review Mayar’s Privacy Policy here: https://mayar.id/kebijakan-privasi .

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. All communications between the Extension and our servers are encrypted using SSL/TLS. We rely on industry-standard security practices provided by Supabase to safeguard your data from unauthorized access.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or through a notice within the Extension.
