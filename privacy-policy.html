Privacy Policy for SHOPER

Effective Date: August 27, 2025

Thank you for using SHOPER ("the Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect and how we use, share, and safeguard it.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of the Extension, we only collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you register for a trial account or log in, we collect your email address and password. Your password is securely stored in an encrypted format.
  • License Information: When you activate a premium license, we maintain a record of that activation linked to your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • User Authentication: To verify your identity during login and secure your account.
  • License Management: To manage your subscription status, whether trial or premium license.
  • Essential Communications: We may use your email address to send important account-related updates, such as payment confirmations, trial expiration reminders, or critical service updates.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

To maximize your privacy, we explicitly do not collect, store, or track the following:

  • Your browsing history on Shopee or any other websites.
  • Products you view, search, or analyze.
  • Personal information from your Shopee account.
  • Any data you input into the extension’s filters.

All research data you perform remains within your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Information Sharing

We will never share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

Your information is securely stored using services provided by Supabase, Inc., our backend infrastructure provider. Supabase adheres to strict security and data privacy standards.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All communication between the Extension and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS. We rely on industry-standard security practices provided by Supabase to safeguard your data from unauthorized access.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify you via email or through a notice within the Extension.
